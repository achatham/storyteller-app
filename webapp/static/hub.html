Uploading runs the initial pass: it reads the whole book, builds the character roster, and splits it into read-aloud pages. In draw-as-you-read mode, page pictures are drawn on demand (the first pages during upload, so it opens instantly). In pre-illustrate mode it stops after the roster so you can review it, then illustrates every page in one background batch at the Batch-API discount (~50% off).
